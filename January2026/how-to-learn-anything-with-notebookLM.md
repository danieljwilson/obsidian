### How to learn subject matter in a field
#### 1. Data ingestion
Uploaded textbooks, research papers, and lecture transcripts on the subject.

#### 2. Questions for NotebookLM
- What are the 5 core mental models that every expert in this field shares?
	- Show me 5 real-world scenarios where these mental models break down or fail, and explain how experts adapt their thinking in those situations.
- Show me the 3 places where experts in this field fundamentally disagree, and what each side's strongest argument is.
- Generate 10 questions that would expose whether someone deeply understands this subject versus someone who just memorized facts.
	- answer those questions using the source material. 
	- Every wrong answer triggers a follow-up: "Explain why this is wrong and what I'm missing."
