### Description
Daily 10 minute routine
**0:00–1:00 — Knee-to-Wall Ankle Dorsiflexion (alternate sides)**
- Cue: keep heel down; drive knee forward over 2nd/3rd toe; smooth reps (don’t bounce hard).
- Video (YouTube): [Knee-to-Wall Ankle Mobility Drill](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y1IZXkdPPdw).

**1:00–3:00 — Half-Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch (1:00/side)**
- Cue: posterior pelvic tilt (“tuck”), glute squeeze on the kneeling side, ribs stacked over pelvis.
- Video (YouTube): [Half-Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch (step-by-step)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gqoPYLUgP48).

**3:00–4:00 — Adductor Rockbacks (30s/side)**
- Cue: neutral spine; sit hips back toward heel; feel inner-thigh lengthening, not low-back rounding.
- Video (YouTube): [Adductor Rock-back](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uZyLZDxcD38).

**4:00–6:00 — World’s Greatest Stretch with Rotation (alternate sides)**
- Cue: long back leg; rotate through upper back; move slowly and own the end range.
- Video (YouTube): [The World’s Greatest Stretch (Mobility Exercise)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-CiWQ2IvY34).

**6:00–7:00 — Dead Bug (controlled reps)**
- Cue: low back gently “heavy” on the floor; exhale as you extend; stop before your back arches.
- Video (YouTube): [How to Do a Dead Bug (PT guide)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GbSC02oU3To).

**7:00–8:30 — Side Plank (45s/side)**
- Cue: shoulder packed; hips forward; top hip stacked (don’t rotate open); steady breathing.
- Video (YouTube): [Side Plank Tutorial (proper form)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iNbH7_edNI8).

**8:30–9:30 — Bird Dog (alternate sides)**
- Cue: reach long, not high; keep pelvis level; pause 1–2s each rep.
- Video (YouTube): [Bird Dog (proper form)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZdAHe9_HeEw).

**9:30–10:00 — Glute Bridge March**
- Cue: ribs down; hips stay level as one foot lifts; slow “march,” minimal sway.
- Video (YouTube): [Glute Bridge March (tutorial)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M8LAwv4Od2I).
### Exceptions
-  Not necessary on days I am doing Yoga/Pilates
