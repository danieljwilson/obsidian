## Remote Focus
- [We Work Remotely](https://weworkremotely.com) — Large general remote board; strong for software/engineering; less curated but high volume.
- [Otta](https://otta.com) — Strong matching UX; very good for tech/startups; best coverage UK/EU + some US; quality tends to be higher than average boards.
- [Himalayas](https://himalayas.app) — Remote-first focus; clean filtering (timezone, region, salary where available); solid for engineering/product.
- [Remotive](https://remotive.com) — Curated remote listings + newsletter; good breadth across roles; useful filters for remote-only.
- [Dynamite Jobs](https://dynamitejobs.com) — Remote roles for startups/SMBs; often ops/marketing + some engineering; generally decent quality.
- [Jobgether](https://jobgether.com) — Aggregates and re-lists remote jobs from many sources; high volume, variable quality; good for broad sweeps.
- [Working Nomads](https://www.workingnomads.com) — Aggregator + email alerts; solid for engineering/data; quality varies, but good coverage.
- [SkipTheDrive](https://www.skipthedrive.com) — Mostly aggregated remote listings; useful for quick scans, but expect duplicates and mixed quality.
- [Wellfound (formerly AngelList Talent)](https://wellfound.com) — Best for early-stage startups; strong for ML/AI + product/eng; compensation/equity often visible.
- [Europe Remote Jobs](https://euremotejobs.com/) — EU/timezone-friendly remote roles; smaller board; good if you want CET-compatible positions.
- [Jobspresso](https://jobspresso.co) — More curated; tends to feature reputable remote-first companies; lower volume, higher signal.
