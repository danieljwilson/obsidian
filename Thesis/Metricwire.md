# Notes
From <brian.stewart@metricwire.com>
Currently, we can capture the following sensor data through the Metricwire App:

-   **Activity Type Classifier (Stationary/Walking/Running/Biking/Vehicle)**
-   **GPS Location Tracking**
-   **Pedometer**
-   Gravity
-   Linear Acceleration
-   Accelerometer
-   Gyroscope
-   Magnetometer
-   Altimeter
-   Ambient Light & Temperature (Android only)

**Most Popular in Bold**

I confirmed with the engineering team that the forthcoming wearable/fitness tracker integration will cover data captured by Apple Health (iPhone 4S+) & Google Fit apps.

-   Apple Health/Watch/iHealth devices (BPM, Glucometer, Scale, etc)
-   Oura Ring
-   Fitibit (covers about 24 trackers/devices)
-   Withings (covers 27 devices)
-   Garmin (covers 82 devices)
-   MyFitness Pal/Underarmour (10 devices) 
-   Google Fit App & ~ 50 Smart watches
-   A few more miscellaneous ones

Jeff can get you set up with demo accounts to evaluate the platform. Just pass over names/emails for the accounts and any protocol documentation you'd like to see incorporated.
