# Notes
>methods and proposed analyses are pre-registered and peer reviewed prior to research being conducted

>designed to minimize publication bias and research bias in hypothesis-driven research, while also allowing the flexibility to conduct exploratory (unregistered) analyses and report serendipitous findings.

#### Initial Submission
1. key research question
2. background literature
3. hypotheses
4. experimental procedures
5. analysis pipeline
6. a sampling plan (statistical power analysis or Bayesian equivalent)
7. pilot data (where applicable).
---
**Authors are reminded that any deviation from the stated experimental procedures, regardless of how minor it may seem to the authors, could lead to rejection of the manuscript at Stage 2**
