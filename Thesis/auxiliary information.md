# Notes

- critical for making estimates from probability samples with [[nonresponse]] and from non-probability samples.
- can use [[auxiliary information]] to create stratification after sample has been collected ([[post-stratification]])

**EXAMPLE**
If you collected a random sample of people from the USA you could then weight the responses in each state appropriately if you knew the state population. 

- 10 people from Florida and 10 people from Massachusetts in the sample
- population of Florida = 21m, population Massachusetts = 7m...therefore people from Florida should get 3x the weight in trying to create a nationally representative statistic
