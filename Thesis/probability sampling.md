# Notes
All members of the [[target population]] have a known, nonzero probability of being sampled, and all people who are sampled respond to the survey.
- These conditions rarely met in the "real" world...
	- Generally there will be [[coverage error]]/[[coverage bias]] and [[nonresponse bias]]
