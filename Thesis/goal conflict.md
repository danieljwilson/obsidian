# Notes
When the pursuit of one goal detracts from the pursuit of another.

- structurally conflict occurs between horizontally linked goals (same level) within a hierarchical structure (Emmons,  1989)
- subgoals can also conflict with higher order goals
	- e.g. the means justify the end...
- compromised performance on two competing goals may mean that neither is satisfied
