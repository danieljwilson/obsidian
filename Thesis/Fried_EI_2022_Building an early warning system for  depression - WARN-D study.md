##### Design Principles
1. **personalized early warning system**
2. **focus on students**
	- MDD is highly  heterogeneous  in  terms  of  etiology  and  problems  people  experience (Fried  et  al.,  2022;  Kendler,  2012a;  Zimmerman  et  al.,  2014),  and  efforts  to  understand  and  predict  depression onset are more likely to succeed in more homogeneous populations (Cai et al.,  2015, 2020).
3. **real world feasibility**
	- doesn't take place in lab
	- leverages smartphone/device
4. **generic infrastructure**
	- can be transferred and applied  to many other disorders and target populations.
5. **open scholarship principles**

PAYMENT
>We pay participants up to 90€ for completing all surveys in Stage 1 (7.50€), Stage 2  (45€), and Stage 3 (37.50€). For the participants we plan to invite for Stage 4, completing all  surveys provides another 45€. Further,  participants  who complete  the  1-year  and  2-year  follow-up surveys in  Stage 3 can participate in 500€ lotteries for  each  survey.
