# Notes
[K-nearest neighbors](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K-nearest_neighbors_algorithm) is a classification (or regression) algorithm that in order to determine the classification of a point, combines the classification of the K nearest points. 

- It is supervised because you are trying to classify a point based on the known classification of other points.
