# Notes
Suggested that people are motivated by [a hierarchy of needs](https://www.verywellmind.com/what-is-maslows-hierarchy-of-needs-4136760).^[[paper](https://www.jstor.org/stable/10.5406/amerjpsyc.126.2.0155?seq=1)]

 The most basic needs are centered on things necessary for life such as food and water, but as people move up the hierarchy these needs become centered on things such as esteem and self-actualization.
