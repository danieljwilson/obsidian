# Notes
Evaluations people make about objects, ideas, events, or other people.

- can be positive or negative
- [[explicit attitude]]: conscious beliefs that can guide [[decision]]s and [[behavior]].
- [[implicit attitudes]]: unconscious beliefs that still influence [[decision]]s and [[behavior]]^[So they have no difference in effect??].

**Three components**
1. cognitive
2. emotional
3. behavioral

**Three dimensions**
1. [[attitude strength]]
2. [[attitude accessibility]]
3. [[attitude ambivalence]]
