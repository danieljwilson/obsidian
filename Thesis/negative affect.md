# Notes
Arises when there is a discrepancy between one's current state and one's goals or expectations

- Individuals with low [[negative affect]] are characterized as “being in a state of calmness and serenity”
- those with high NA are characterized by “subjective distress and unpleasurable engagement” which includes apprehension, anger, irritation, shame, fear, sadness, guilt, and a negative view of the self
