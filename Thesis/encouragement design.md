# Notes
Situation where a treatment is randomly assigned which then increases the likelihood (i.e. encourages) of a primary treatment of interest.

**EXAMPLE**
The Vietnam draft could be considered a randomly assigned secondary treatment that encouraged some people to take the primary treatment, which was serving in the military.
