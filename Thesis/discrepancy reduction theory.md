# Notes
People compare their actual state with salient reference values (goals). 

If the comparison indicates a discrepancy, people adjust their behavior to reduce the gap. This feedback process, the action-monitoring loop, controls the intentional behavior.

- Emotions are seen as direct indicators of this discrepancy reducing process.
- Goal progress is the proximate cause of emotional [[subjective well-being]].
