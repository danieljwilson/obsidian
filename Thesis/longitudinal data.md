# Notes
Entity under investigation is observed repeatedly as it exists and evolves over time.

- temporal precedence is necessary but not sufficient condition for [[causality]]
	- e.g. higher stress -> higher negative affect
	- could be a [[common cause principle|common cause]]
- Allows us to isolate intra-individual variability and change
	- can explicitly examine between-person differences in within-person change...
- Can also consider within-person processes
