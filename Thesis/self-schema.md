# Notes
Refers to people’s self-definitions in domains of enduring investment and concern

**ALT**
Sets of cognitions that guide behavior

- characteristically measured by ratings of the descriptiveness and importance of traits related to the behavioral domain of interest (e.g., ratings of “physically active” and “keeps in shape’’ have been used to measure exercise self-schemas, Kendzierski, 1990b)
- idea for [[intention-behavior gap]] is that gap should be lower for domains that the subject finds highly self-descriptive and highly important^[Seems like the idea is basically does this behavior/goal align with my identity?]
