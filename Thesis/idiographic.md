[[Immanuel Kant]]
[[nomothetic]]

# Notes
describes the effort to understand the meaning of **contingent**, unique, and often cultural, or subjective phenomena.

- [[nomothetic]] and [[idiographic]] are terms used by Neo-Kantian philosopher [[Wilhelm Windelband]] to describe two distinct approaches to knowledge, each one corresponding to a different intellectual tendency.
- often [[qualitative]] (whereas [[nomothetic]] approaches are [[quantitative]])
