# Notes

## 11 | Sample Size
The total [[sample size]] depends on:
1. **number of participants**
2. **number of measured variables**
3. **number of time points** in which variables are measured for each participant

> not much is known about statistical power in intensive longitudinal designs

- [[statistical power]] informs [[sample size]]
- To conduct power analysis for complex research questions simulation-based power analysis may be best (specifically talking about [[multilevel model]]s)
- One problem with simulation-based power analysis is that it doesn't necessarily account for the fact that repeated measures within individuals are likely to be correlated...
	- *PowerAnalysisIL*^[Lafit et al., 2021] attempts to account for temporal dependencies...
