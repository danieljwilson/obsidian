# Notes
Relates to personal control and the extent to which you perceive that you have control over your environment.

**ALT**
The degree to which one generally perceives  events to be under their control (internal locus) or under the control of powerful others (Rotter, 1996, 1990)

**Internal**
- Feel like you make your own fate/luck

**External**
- Feel like you are floating along guided by forces beyond you control
