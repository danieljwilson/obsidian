# Notes
Variables that are not directly observed but are rather inferred from other variables that are observed.

- most social scientific concepts are not directly observable (e.g. intelligence, social capital)
- when attempting to measure latent construct there is [[measurement error]]
	- [[systematic error]] (mean ≠ 0)
	- [[random error]] (mean = 0)
