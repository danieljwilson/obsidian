# Notes
Projective test that presents the subject with a series of ambiguous pictures, and the subject is asked to develop a spontaneous story for each picture. The assumption is that the subject will project his or her own needs into the story and these will reflect certain underlying themes.

- Used to explore the underlying dynamics of personality such as internal conflicts, dominant drives, interests, and motives.
- scoring system developed by McClelland and Atkinson measures an individual's score for each of the needs of achievement, affiliation, and power
- the only tool that has been found to measure implicit motivation with any degree of validity
