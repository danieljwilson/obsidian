# Notes
Experiments normally measure the ==average effect==, but the effect is probably **not the same for everyone**...


**EXAMPLE**
Schultz et al. (2007) ran an experiment on power consumption. Looked like intervention had no effect but basically people told they were using more than others **reduced** consumption and people that were told they were using less than others **increased** consumption...
