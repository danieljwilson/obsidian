# Notes
Reflects the tendency to follow through with promises to others and follow rules that make social groups work more smoothly.

- On the negative end of the spectrum it reflects the tendency to be an unreliable partner in achievement settings and to break one’s promises.
- tends to correlate quite highly with [[agreeableness]]
