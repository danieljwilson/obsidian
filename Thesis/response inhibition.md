# Notes
A cognitive process and more specifically, an [[executive control|executive function]] – that permits an individual to **inhibit** their impulses and natural, habitual, or dominant behavioral **responses** to stimuli ( a.k.a. [[prepotent response|prepotent responses]]) in order to select a more appropriate behavior consistent with completing their [[goals]].

- Fundamental [[control mechanism]]
- Necessary part of modifying actions as goals change (e.g. in response to changes in environment/context)
