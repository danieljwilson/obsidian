[[NYCgov poverty threshold]]

# Notes
Nationwide threshold based on distribution of expenditures on:
- food
- clothing
- shelter
- utilities

Based on reference unit composed of a **two-adult**, **two-child** family. 

- Expenditures are measured using a three-year moving average of data available in the Bureau of Labor Statistics’ Consumer Expenditure Survey (CE).
- An additional factor is included in the base threshold to account for miscellaneous expenses, such as non-work-related travel, household supplies, and personal care products.
