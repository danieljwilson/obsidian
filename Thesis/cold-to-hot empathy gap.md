# Notes
Occurs when someone is in a **cold** (emotionally neutral) state, and has trouble understanding someone in a **hot** (emotional) state, generally as a result of underestimating the influence that emotions, impulses, and visceral drives have on the individual in the **hot** state

- setting goals in a cold (e.g., calm or satiated) state can reduce goal attainment because the impact of visceral states (e.g., arousal, hunger) are not taken into account
