# Notes
**Two ways in which behavior can be influenced**
Descriptive norms (what are others doing)
Social norms (what are others thinking)

Three factors that can influence individual reaction to COVID safety measures:
- Vulnerability
- Adherence (I think at the larger friend/community/national level)
- Approval

Also a "fusion" factor related to how closely an individual feels connected to their country.

More severe rules could lead to higher levels of well-being...(less time spent thinking/worrying?)

Higher well-being for higher compliers - could this just be intention-behavior gap at work? i.e. maybe you are not capturing a difference in intention...
