# Notes
Core of nudging is change in ==CHOICE ARCHITECTURE==

Not so much based on **INCENTIVES**

**Nudging not trying to change the mind (no learning), trying to change BEHAVIOR.**
- Architectural nudges
	- default, friction, positional effects
- Educative nudges (not about competence or skill)
	- warnings, reminders, and disclosure of information
