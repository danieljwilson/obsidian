### Conclusion

"Measurement Matters" presents a compelling case for the inclusion of personal quality assessments alongside traditional cognitive ability measures in educational settings. By providing a nuanced look at the methodologies, challenges, and implications of measuring these attributes, Duckworth and Yeager contribute valuable insights to the broader conversation on education and student success. Their work emphasizes the complex, multifaceted nature of student achievement and the importance of a more holistic approach to education.
