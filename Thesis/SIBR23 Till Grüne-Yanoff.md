### Normative assessment
What does autonomy actually mean?

autonomy is decreased if:
- An intervention increases the incoherence if an agents actions
- Reduces the ability of an agent to engage in practical reasoning about their actions
- Reduces ability to appreciate/understand REASONS an agent has for action
