Perseverance and passion for long-term [[goals]].

- predicts achievement in academic domains
- correlation between grit and [[conscientiousness]] (r = .77; Duckworth & Quinn, 2009)
	- They found some evidence grit uniquely predicted educational attainment when controlling for the Big Five trait
