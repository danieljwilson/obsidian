# Notes
## Summary
Benefit of [[intensive longitudinal data]] is gaining insight into the ==dynamics of a process==

Two promising approaches:
### [[time series analysis]]
>single-subject technique, in which a large number 
of repeated measures from a single system (e.g., a person  or dyad) are analyzed, with a focus on how the current  observation  can  be  predicted  from  preceding  observations  of  the  same  and/or  other  variables.

==Can reveal large individual differences in both the strength and  the  direction  of  relationships between variables.==

### [[dynamic multilevel modeling]]

> based  on  a  time-series  model  at  Level  1  that  describes  the  within-person  process,  while   between-person differences in the dynamic features are  modeled at Level 2

A  sophisticated  method  for  handling  non-equidistant  measurements  is  continuous   time modeling (Oravecz, Tuerlinckx, & Vandekerckhove,  2011; Voelkle, Oud, Davidov, & Schmidt, 2012)
