# Notes

- typically gathered from knowledge-able informants, such as friends
- tend to be internally consistent, with relatively high levels of inter-judge agreement (John & Robins,1993)
- tend to demonstrate equivalent levels of predictive validity to self-reports (e.g.,Connelly & Ones, 2010; Lodi-Smith et al., 2010; Vazire, 2010).
