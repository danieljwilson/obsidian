# Notes
A means to measure the ability to differentiate between information-bearing patterns and random patterns that distract from the information.

-  permits the separation of sensitivity from criterial, decision-making factors
-  key notion of SDT is that human performance in many tasks is limited by variability in the internal representation of stimuli due to internal or external noise
