# Notes
Making inferences from your respondents to your [[target population]].

Easy to make errors:
![[Pasted image 20211115114637.png]]

- Differences between the target population and the frame population are called [[coverage error]]
- A sample with different characteristics than the overall [[frame population]] can introduce [[sampling error]]

**EXAMPLE**
Error of representation when Reader's Digest conducted straw poll for 1936 US general election. Got it wrong as due to a [[nonresponse bias]]
