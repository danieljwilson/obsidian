## Summary
**5 factors that could help the practitioner understand the  patient intention-behavior gap:** 
1. the motivation
2. the trigger
3. the  response
4. the capacity
5. the process

>[!Note]
>Need to **understand difficulties**^[and I am suggesting these should ideally be understood as close to the level of the individual as possible] in order to apply strategies (interventions)

Focuses on [[lifestyle factors]], such as:
- physical activity
- healthy eating
- maintaining a healthy weight
- not smoking

Idea is that:
- we have the knowledge and theoretical ability to control these factors at the individual level
- success would have a major impact on leading chronic diseases

>[!Quote]
>Lifestyle is medicine.
