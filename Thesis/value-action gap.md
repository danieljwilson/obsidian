# Notes
The space that occurs when the values (personal and cultural) or attitudes of an individual do not correlate to their actions.

- also called the _attitude-behavior gap_, _intention-behavior gap_, _KAP-gap_ (knowledge-attitudes-practice gap) or _belief-behavior gap_)[\[1\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Value-action_gap#cite_note-Kollmuss2-1)[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Value-action_gap#cite_note-2)[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Value-action_gap#cite_note-3)
- often related to attitudes and behaviors surrounding environmental issues

### Thoughts
Seems like a less granular version of the [[value-intention-behavior gap]], focusing on the endpoints.
