# Notes
A macro theory of human motivation and personality that concerns people's inherent growth tendencies and innate psychological needs. It is concerned with the [[motivation]] behind choices people make **without** external influence and interference. 
- focuses on the degree to which human behavior is self-motivated and self-determined
