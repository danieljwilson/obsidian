# Notes
Data that results from observing a social system **without intervening** in some way.

Can be thought of roughly as everything that does not involve talking with people(e.g., surveys) or changing people’s environments (e.g., experiments). Thus, in addition to business and government records, observational data also includes things like the text of newspaper articles and satellite photos.
