#### Empowering citizens with behavioral science

#### [[nudging|Nudging]]
Core of nudging is change in ==CHOICE ARCHITECTURE==

Not so much based on **INCENTIVES**

**Nudging not trying to change the mind (no learning), trying to change BEHAVIOR.**
- Architectural nudges
	- default, friction, positional effects
- Educative nudges (not about competence or skill)
	- warnings, reminders, and disclosure of information

- Nudging is not a panacea

#### [[behavioral boosting|Boosting]]
[[behavioral boosting]] DOES involve learning
- decision by yourself, for yourself
- as soon as nudge disappears, then the behavior would return to baseline (unless habit has taken hold)
- in principle implied effect of a boost (if successful) could endure
- does require cooperation/engagement, as opposed to [[nudging]]
	- therefore does not violate autonomy/transparency

Idea of "==critical ignoring=="
