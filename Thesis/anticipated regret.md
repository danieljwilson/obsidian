[[intention-behavior gap]]

# Notes
Refers to beliefs about the extent of regret, tension, or upset one would feel if they did not perform a particular behavior.

- has strong associations with intentions to perform behaviors

**Note**: doesn't seem to be a good scale to measure this. There is the [[regret scale]] ([link](http://www.sjdm.org/dmidi/Regret_Scale.html)), but that is not focused on ==anticipated== regret...
