# Notes
[[demand effects]] refers to an experimental artifact where participants form an interpretation of the experiment's purpose and subconsciously change their behavior to fit that interpretation.

The [[demand effects|Hawthorne effect]] is a type of reactivity in which individuals modify an aspect of their behavior in response to their awareness of being observed.
