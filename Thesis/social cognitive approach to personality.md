# Notes
Emphasizes the **interaction** between our [[trait|traits]] and their **social context**.

**ALT**
emphasizes the importance of [observational learning](https://www.verywellmind.com/what-is-observational-learning-2795402), self-efficacy, situational influences, and cognitive processes.

- What is the actual cause of a behavior? Personality? Context?

- Originally proposed by [[Albert Bandura]].
	- He referred to this phenomenon as [[reciprocal determinism]]
	- You choose to be in certain environments based on your personality and then the environments in turn reinforce/influence your personality
	- we are both the creators and products of the situations we put ourselves in...

Key indicator of personality in this school of thought is our sense of personal control, or our [[locus of control]].


Can be called **person-situation controversy**...

George Kelly: "personal constructs" re: how world works
Walter Mischel: "person variables"
- competencies
- encoding strategies
- expectancies
- values
- plans
