# Notes
**Equation**
$A = A_0e^{rt}$
- $A =$ the amount at time $t$, or the final amount
- $A_0 =$ the amount at time $t = 0$, or the initial amount
- $e =$ a constant, [[Euler's number]]
- $r =$ the relative rate of growth ($r > 0$) or decay ($r < 0$)
- $t =$ time
