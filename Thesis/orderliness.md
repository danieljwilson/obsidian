# Notes
Encompasses the overarching tendency to be **prepared**.

- A spectrum
- Positive side includes tendencies toward:
	- neatness
	- cleanliness
	- planfulness 
- Negative side includes tendencies toward:
	- disorderliness
	- disorganization
	- messiness

Often believed to be one of two main components of [[conscientiousness]], along with [[industriousness]].
