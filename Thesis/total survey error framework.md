# Notes

Total survey error = representation errors + measurement errors

**Two ==TYPES== of error**:
1. [[bias]] (systematic)
2. [[variance]] (random)

**Two ==SOURCES== of error**:
1. [[representation (survey)]] (who you sample)
2. [[measures|measurement]] (what you infer from your instrument)
