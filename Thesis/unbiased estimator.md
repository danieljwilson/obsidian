# Notes
Characteristic that in repeated samples the [[parameter]] estimate error averages out to zero^[Not sure it has to be a parameter...] as the deviations from the true value are **not systematically up or down**.

- distinct from the [[law of large numbers]], which says that the sample mean gets closer to the population mean as the sample size grows
- unbiasedness is not affected by sample size...
