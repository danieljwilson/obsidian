# Notes
Restricts the treated population to those newly initiating treatment
- align start time to the start of treatment
- can combine with [[active comparator]] design where you have the second group doing something ELSE...(yoga/zumba, different drugs)
	- in some cases we may WANT to compare to unexposed
- in some cases you can't do this as there is not time of no treatment (e..g air polution)

**EXAMPLE**
If we are interested in the effect of yoga on blood pressure we compare the treatment group only to people who are not currently practicing yoga but who have also NEVER done yoga in the past...
