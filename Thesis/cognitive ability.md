# Notes
Mental capability that … involves the ability to reason, plan, solve problems, think abstractly, comprehend complex ideas, learn quickly and learn from experience. (Gottfredson, 1997, p. 13)

- sometimes referred to as [[intelligence|general intelligence]]
- supports the ability to comprehend situations, figure out what is needed, and plan a course of action
-  closely associated with educational attainment, occupation, and health outcomes (Plomin & Von Stumm, 2018)
