# Notes
Researcher intervenes in the world and then measures an outcome.

**Alt**
Any activity that produces or observes an outcome.

- "perturb and observe"
- enable researchers to move beyond the [[correlation]]s in [[naturally occurring data]] in order to reliably answer certain [[causality|cause-and-effect]] questions
- Examples are flipping a coin, rolling a 6-sided die, or trying a new route to work to see if it’s faster than the old route.

**Keys to rich experiments**^["simple experiments" focus on answering a specific question: what is the average effect of this specific treatment with this specific implementation for this population of participants at this time?]
