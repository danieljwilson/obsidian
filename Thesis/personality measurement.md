# Notes
Question who/what is the **self**?

Two major theories in 20th century:
1. [[trait]]
	- Stable and lasting behavior patterns and conscious motivations. Predict [[attitude]] and [[behavior]]. But can be context dependent (shy person singing Karaoke at office party)
	- believe we can access personality [[trait|traits]] by having people answer a series of **test questions**...(e.g. personality trait inventories, e.g. [[Big Five]]).

2. [[social cognitive approach to personality]]
3. [[humanistic theory]] (e.g. [[Abraham Maslow]])
	- Often reject standardized measurements
	- Measure self-concept...
	- Look at gap between how people would like to be and how they actually are



Many historical methods:
- Rorschach
- Dream analysys (Freud)
- Free association
- Psychodynamic camp
	- thematic aperception test
