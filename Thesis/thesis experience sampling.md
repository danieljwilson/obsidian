### SensorKit
From [[Apple SensorKit]] ([link](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/sensorkit?changes=latest_minor))^[Many of these seem great - curious if they work with RKStudio...maybe could pay for implementation]
[`class SRKeyboardMetrics`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/sensorkit/srkeyboardmetrics?changes=latest_minor)
The configuration of a device’s keyboard and its usage patterns.

[`class SRDeviceUsageReport`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/sensorkit/srdeviceusagereport?changes=latest_minor)
The frequency and relative duration that the user uses their device, particular Apple apps, or websites.

[`class SRMessagesUsageReport`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/sensorkit/srmessagesusagereport?changes=latest_minor)
An object that describes the user’s Messages app activity over a period of time.

[`class SRPhoneUsageReport`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/sensorkit/srphoneusagereport?changes=latest_minor)
An object that describes the user’s phone activity over a period of time.
